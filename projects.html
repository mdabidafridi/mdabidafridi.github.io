Trip Tracker app for collecting data of users

L3 T2 (6th semester) [Demo Video]

Technologies: Flutter, MongoDB

When the app launches for the first time, it will seek location permission and a userID. The app will remember this userID. When the user goes out, he/she will click on the 'Start Trip' button and select a 'Mode.' When the trip ends, the user should press the 'End Trip' button, and then the data will be sent to MongoDB. It will collect data on location, time, and mode.
